Command List:
'.' is necessary as prefix of every commands!
.start - to enter your name in registry and further playing!
.archer - to change your class to archer
.warrior - to change your class to warrior
.mage - to change your class to mage
.attack - to attack on enemies
.evade - to evade the enemies
.stat - to see your current progress, and current data
